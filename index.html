My ten years of Software Development

Published: 2016-04-02

Next week I am celebrating my tenth anniversary of career as a software Engineer...

Whatever career you may choose for yourself - doctor, lawyer, teacher - let me propose an avocation to be pursued along with it. Become a dedicated fighter for civil rights. Make it a central part of your life.

It will make you a better doctor, a better lawyer, a better teacher. It will enrich your spirit as nothing else possibly can. It will give you that rare sense of nobility that can only spring from love and selflessly helping your fellow man. Make a career of humanity. Commit yourself to the noble struggle for human rights. You will make a greater person of yourself, a greater Nation of your country and a finer world to live in.

The Papers of Martin Luther King, Jr: Threshold of a new decade, January 1959-December 1960, King Jr., Martin Luther; Clayborne Carson; et al. (2005).
